Return to reality.

But for me, I may have no friends, no relatives, but a purpose for myself is never lacking. I looked back to the past to see what I liked and what I was good at. During one year of high school, I have been looking for a path to go myself. I am very good at drawing, but I do not really affirm it if I say it is a passion. Computer science is something I've been exposed to very early on, at least in the place where I was born. I can understand the principles that operate and how to program simple software. However, I used to be very frustrated with programming when what I encountered was just a screen and lines of code, nothing more, nothing less.

 
 
 
 
 

Milestones

Anything changed when I became a student in an Introduction of Information Technology class in my senior year of high school. I experienced small projects like the Sphero Dance and Game Development with Scratch from MIT. Programming is not unfamiliar to me, but when my products are being used with interest by people and produced artwork, that makes me very happy.

 
 
 

I’m very shy to show my face :0

 

And yeah. You can say that it was a milestone. I am not sure if the path I take can turn the other direction in the future. But now, Computer Science is what I am after and I will try my best to achieve my dream.

It a pleasure to know you. I look forward to working and getting to know you better.
